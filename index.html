About Us

The JIIT Lost & Found service is dedicated to helping students and staff recover their lost belongings. Our mission is to provide a seamless experience for reporting and retrieving lost items within the campus.

We understand how important your belongings are, and we strive to ensure that every found item is safely returned to its rightful owner. Our platform allows users to quickly report found items, search through lost items, and connect with others who may have found their belongings.

Join us in creating a community of trust and support where everyone can easily find their lost possessions.
